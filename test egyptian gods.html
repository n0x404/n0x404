Rê

He's literally the sun which appear under different forms. At the beggining of the day he's Khepri, a winged scarab pushing the sun. During the day time he's which is also often associated with Horus the elder.In the evening he's Atoum often seen as an old man with a cane. During the night time the sun is recovering energy to appear the next day. He's travelling in a rowboat escorted by diverse divinities.

Shou

Also called Chou, he represent the air, the void separating the earth, Geb, and the sky, Nout, from procreating. Him and his sister Tefnout are the first hypostasis of the demiurge, the creator. He completes the demiurge with his sister, they're Atoum's twins.

Tefnout

She's born from a spit of Atoum. She completes the demiurge with her brother, they're Atoum's twins. They personalized the life and balance necessary to the fullfillment of Atoum.

Geb

He personalises Earth, father of the god since he's the first with Nout to procreate in a sexual way. His children are :

  • Osiris
  • Horus the elder
  • Seth
  • Isis
  • Nephtys
He's mad at Nout for eatiing his other children, the stars. He also welcomes the dead and will become a successor of Rê. His successor will be Osiris.

Nout

She gave birth to the stars that she ate in the evening. this is why she's often represented as a sow. She asked Rê to give her the shape of a cow. She then had bring him to the celestial space. She gave life to five gods during five days (ref geb). She's often linked to the sarcophagus.

Osiris

He's the first born of his sibling. He became the king of Egypt, or Earth and taught agriculture and civilisation to the human kind. he was murdered by his brother Seth. His wife Isis reassembled him and resurrected him just the time to make a child, Horus the younger. Osiris then became god of the underworld. He also sybolized death, resurrection and the cycle of Nile floods.

Isis

She's born the fourth day after all her brothers and she's a magic expert. She's mostly represented as the loyal wife of Osiris and as a mother. She will go on a quest to resuscitate her husband by rassembling all his parts with the help of Nephtys, her sister. At this moment she will bear Osiris's child , Horus the younger.
